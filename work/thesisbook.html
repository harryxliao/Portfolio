OVERVIEW

Dream Reality is an MFA thesis book completed at MassArt's Dynamic Media Institute, exploring the profound phenomenon of lucid dreaming. Through a synergy of thesis writing, art installations, and interactive design, it translates conscious dream narratives into tangible experiences — challenging readers to examine the blurred boundaries between inner perception and waking reality. Designed with InDesign, Photoshop, and custom typography.
