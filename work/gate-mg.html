01

Social Motion Design

Independently designed four high-quality social motion visuals for advertising campaigns, blending brand identity with dynamic storytelling to drive social engagement and brand awareness.

02

Cross-Functional Collaboration

Cross-collaborated with visual and video teams, rapidly iterating to align creative direction with business goals — delivering multiple high-pressure, high-spec projects on time.
